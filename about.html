About Me

I have been interested in computers and technology since high school. In college I took my first programming class and wasn't all that intrigued at the time, maybe something to do with the course itself.

In early 2018, I was looking into a career change and decided to check out programming and web development again. I signed up for a free online code school(freecodecamp.org) and since then I have been hooked.

After a few months of that I wanted a little more structured eductaion so I gave TeamTreehouse a trial and really enjoyed it. I have since completed their FullStack JavaScript track and many other related courses.

While learning I have built dozens of projects for fun each one becoming more complex as I learn more frameworks and libraries. Most of these can be see on my GitHub account or here on My Work page.

I still love learing new technologies as much as I did when I started this and can't wait to see what comes next!
